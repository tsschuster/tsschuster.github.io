About

I am a theoretical physicist working at the intersection of quantum information science and many-body physics.

I received my Ph.D. in Physics from UC Berkeley in December 2022, working with Professor Norman Y. Yao.

In Fall 2023, I will begin as a Sherman Fairchild Postdoctoral Scholar at the Walter Burke Institute for Theoretical Physics at Caltech.

Here is my CV and a link to my Google Scholar profile.
